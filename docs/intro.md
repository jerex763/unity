# Unity 是什么? / What is Unity?

*一页纸介绍,写给不写代码的人 / A one-page intro for non-technical readers*

---

## 中文

### 这是什么?

Unity 是我们教会自己做的一个手机小程序(网页版,不用下载安装),帮助牧者和同工把"关心人"这件事做得更好。

### 为什么要做它?

现在教会的日常运转散落在各处:

- **活动报名**靠在 WhatsApp 群里反复编辑一条"1. 张三 2. 李四 3. ..."的接龙,改一次发一次,名单经常对不上
- **新朋友来了一次就再也没来**,没人记得跟进,不是不想关心,是没有一个地方记着"该联系谁"
- **成员信息**在各人手机里、各种表格里,想找"住在附近、对英语角有兴趣的朋友"根本无从查起

### 它能做什么?

三件事:

1. **通讯录** — 教会成员和新朋友的信息放在一个安全的地方,能按小组、按状态查找
2. **新人跟进** — 新朋友来访后自动进入跟进清单,同工认领、记录联系情况,确保没有人被遗忘
3. **活动报名和签到** — 打开链接点一下就报名(包括"需要接送"选项),到场扫码签到,再也不用编辑接龙

### 它不做什么?(我们的承诺)

- **不碰钱** — 奉献走教会现有渠道,这个软件不处理任何付款
- **不用人脸识别** — 签到用二维码就够了
- **不监控聊天** — 不会扫描或分析任何人的私人消息
- **不收集敏感信息** — 不问种族;牧养记录只有牧者能看,普通同工看不到

### 现在到哪一步了?

设计已完成,几位弟兄姊妹正在一起开发。进度公开在:https://github.com/jerex763/unity

## English

### What is this?

Unity is a small web app our church is building for ourselves (works in any phone browser, nothing to install). It helps pastors and volunteers do one thing better: caring for people.

### Why build it?

Right now, day-to-day church life is scattered everywhere:

- **Event signups** happen by editing a numbered list in a WhatsApp group — "1. Alex 2. Sam 3. ..." — re-posted every time someone joins, and the list is often wrong
- **Newcomers visit once and never return**, and nobody remembers to follow up. It's not that we don't care — there's just no single place that remembers *who to contact*
- **Member information** lives in personal phones and scattered spreadsheets. Finding "friends who live nearby and are interested in English Corner" is simply impossible

### What does it do?

Three things:

1. **Directory** — members' and visitors' info in one safe place, searchable by group and status
2. **Newcomer follow-up** — after a first visit, the person automatically appears on a follow-up list; a volunteer claims them, logs the contact, and nobody falls through the cracks
3. **Event signup & check-in** — tap a link to register (including a "need a lift" option), scan a QR code at the door. No more editing numbered lists

### What it will NOT do (our commitments)

- **No money** — giving stays with the church's existing channels; this app never handles payments
- **No facial recognition** — a QR code is enough
- **No message monitoring** — it never scans or analyses anyone's private chats
- **No sensitive data collection** — we don't ask about ethnicity; pastoral care notes are visible to pastors only, not to general volunteers

### Where are we now?

Design is complete and a small team from church is building it together. Progress is public at: https://github.com/jerex763/unity
