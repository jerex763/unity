# Short privacy notice / 简短隐私声明

**Version:** `2026-07-draft`

**Status:** Draft for church and legal/privacy review — not approved for
production use. Replace every bracketed placeholder before publication.

## English

**Who we are.** [Church name] uses Unity to support church administration,
community connection and pastoral care. Questions or complaints can be sent to
[privacy contact and email].

**What we collect.** We may record your name, contact details, household
connections, church participation, event attendance and information you choose
to share about faith or pastoral care. Faith and care information is sensitive
and is available only to roles that need it.

**Why and how we use it.** We use this information to keep in contact, coordinate
church activities, follow up requests and provide appropriate pastoral support.
We do not sell it or use it for commercial advertising. We disclose it only to
authorized church workers and service providers needed to operate Unity, unless
you agree otherwise or disclosure is required or permitted by law.

**Your choice and rights.** Please ask us if you want to see or correct your
information, change or withdraw a consent decision, or make a privacy complaint.
Declining or withdrawing consent does not stop you attending church, although it
may limit activities that require us to keep your details. We protect records
with role-based access and retain them only as long as needed under the church's
approved retention policy.

**Consent.** I have had the opportunity to read this notice and ask questions. I
understand the specific purposes above and voluntarily agree to [clearly describe
the collection/use that requires consent]. I understand I may withdraw this
consent for future handling by contacting [privacy contact].

## 中文

**我们是谁。** [教会名称] 使用 Unity 协助教会行政、群体联系及牧养关怀。如有问题或投诉，请联系
[隐私负责人及电子邮箱]。

**我们收集什么。** 我们可能记录您的姓名、联系方式、家庭关系、教会参与情况、活动出席记录，以及您自愿分享的
信仰或牧养关怀资料。信仰与关怀资料属于敏感信息，只有因职责确有需要的人员才可查看。

**使用目的与方式。** 我们使用这些资料与您保持联系、安排教会活动、跟进请求，并提供合适的牧养支持。我们不会
出售您的资料，也不会将其用于商业广告。除非您另行同意，或法律要求或允许披露，否则资料只会提供给获授权的
教会同工，以及运行 Unity 所必需的服务供应商。

**您的选择与权利。** 如需查阅或更正资料、变更或撤回同意，或提出隐私投诉，请联系我们。拒绝或撤回同意不会
阻止您参加教会，但可能限制必须保存个人资料才能提供的活动。我们通过按角色控制访问来保护记录，并只依照教会
批准的保留政策，在必要期限内保存资料。

**同意。** 我已有机会阅读本声明并提出问题。我了解上述具体目的，并自愿同意
[清楚说明需要取得同意的收集或使用事项]。我了解可联系 [隐私负责人]，撤回对未来资料处理的同意。

## Review checklist

- Confirm the church legal/entity name and privacy contact.
- Specify hosting location, service providers and any likely overseas disclosure.
- Link the full privacy policy, complaint process and approved retention schedule.
- Separate optional sensitive-information consent from routine administration.
- Confirm the final wording is informed, voluntary, current, specific and
  understandable for the intended audience.
